Sailor Moon Crystal Overview

Sailor Moon Crystal is a new anime series orginally created in Japan. The story is about a teenage girl named Usagi, who receives magical powers which makes her a sailor soldier named Sailor Moon. Sailor Moon along with her friends defeat evil beings from other planets.

Sailor Moon was originally created by Naoko Takeuchi. Naoko created Sailor Moon in the form of a manga (Japanese Comics), but later was made into an anime (Japanese Cartoon) from 1992-1997. There have been movies, a live action, video games, merchandise, and musicals dedicated to Sailor Moon. In 2014, Naoko wanted to re-vamp the series to be more like her original manga so Sailor Moon Crystal was born and is still airing today.
