Usagi/Sailor Moon

Usagi starts out as a crybaby who does not do well in school. One day a cat named Luna awakens Usagi as a warrior named Sailor Moon who fights for love and justice against evil beings. She can be a little ditzy at times, but has a heart of gold and is always ready to protect her family and friends from anything that comes their way. In a past life, Usagi was the moon princess named Princess Serenity.

Ami/Sailor Mercury

Ami is a genius who goes to the same school as Usagi. Ami is the sailor soldier, Sailor Mercury, with the power of water. She loves to study and is the one who everyone goes to for analyzing the evil beings for weaknesses. In a past life, Ami was princess of the planet Mercury and her job was to protect Princess Serenity.

Rei/Sailor Mars

Rei is a priestess who works at the local shrine. She is the sailor soldier, Sailor Mars with the power of fire. Rei has a serious personality and has psychic abilities to detect an enemy. In her past life she was princess of the planet Mars and she also protected Princess Serenity.

Makoto/Sailor Jupiter

Makoto is a tall girl who transferred to Usagi's school for getting into too many fights at her old school. She is Sailor Jupiter with the power of thunder and lightning. Makoto loves to cook and has a weakness for cute boys. In her past life, she was princess of Jupiter who protected Princess Serenity.

Minako/Sailor Venus

Minako is a fun loving girl who is the leader, after Sailor Moon, of the sailor soldiers. As Sailor Venus she has the powers of love, but don't be fooled her powers are to be reckoned with. Minako has more experience as a sailor soldier because she, under the codename Sailor V, fought enemies before the other soldiers were awakened with their powers. Minako loves to chase after famous idols and can be boy crazy. She was the princess of planet Venus and lead the other soldiers in protected Princess Serenity.

Mamoru Chiba/Tuxedo Kamen

Mamoru is a high school student who meets Usagi and has an instant connection with her. He turns into Tuxedo Kamen to protect sailor moon whenever she is in battle. Mamoru is a serious guy who used to be Prince Endymion of the earth in his past life. As prince, he fell in love with Princess Serenity and they were happy till tragedy struck which led everyone to be reincarnated on earth. Mamoru is in tune with the earth and gets his powers from it. He also has certain psychic abilities that give him hints about enemy forces.

Chibi-Usa/Sailor Chibi Moon

Chibi-Usa is the future daughter of Usagi and Mamoru. She went back in the past for help from her parents' past selves to help with enemies in her current time. Once those enemies were defeated, Chibi-Usa decided to stay in the past to train as the sailor soldier, Sailor Chibi Moon. Her attacks are cute but can be effective.

Luna, Artemis, and Diana

Luna, Artemis, and Diana are the guardian cats whom watch over and guide the sailor soldiers. Luna is a black cat who sticks mainly by Usagi. Artemis is a white cat who mainly sticks with Minako. Diana is the future daughter of Luna and Artemis and is Chibi-Usa's kitten.

Haruka/Sailor Uranus

Haruka is one of the outer soldiers who protects Princess Serenity from a distance as Sailor Uranus. She along with the other outer soldiers are more powerful than Sailor Moon and her soldiers. Haruka likes racecar driving and flirting with pretty girls. Her soulmate is Michiru.

Michiru/Sailor Neptune

Michiru is another outer soldier who protects Princess Serenity along with Haruka as Sailor Neptune. Michiru is a violinist and has the power of the sea by her side. Her soulmate is Haruka.

Setsuna/Sailor Pluto

Setsuna is a mysterious woman who guarded the Door of Space Time for centuries as Sailor Pluto. She is a lonely soldier who always wanted to fight alongside the other sailor soldiers. Her only friend is Chibi-Usa who visits her at the Door of Space Time. Setsuna has the power to stop time, but the consequence of doing that results in her death.

Hotaru/Sailor Saturn

Hotaru is a quiet girl who does not have any friends. She is the soldier of destruction, death, and rebirth, Sailor Saturn. Hotaru is a troubled girl whom Chibi-Usa eventually befriends. She has a father who is influenced by evil and uses Hotaru as a vessel for an evil being.
